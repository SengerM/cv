Other interests, projects and activities

  • HTML scientific documents. Tired of producing and receiving scientific documentation in PDF format, which is nice but has many drawbacks (e.g. the lack of interactivity and not being mobile-friendly), I decided to start using HTML. After learning the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, I developed a framework similar to what LaTeX is for TeX, but for HTML. It allows the writer to easily produce high quality scientific documents in HTML. I am now actively using this development to share my results with colleagues.
