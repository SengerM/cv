Languages Spanish, English.
PhD student in the group of Prof. Ben Kilminster at the University of Zurich. I am working on the development of the so called 4D pixels aiming to measure both time and position of charged particles produced at collider experiments. I work both in the characterization of different types of detector technologies as well as in the development of a readout ASIC for these new pixels.
